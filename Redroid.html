Daemonize Redroid.

Will attempt to datmonize Redroid. Daemonization is the process of putting Redroid in the background as a service. For instance launching Redroid in a terminal will make you lose the terminal, or launching Redroid from a ssh session and closing the session will kill Redroid. A call to this will detach it from the terminal such that the process become a daemon.

Parameters
ircThe instance the daemonization originated from.
channelThe channel the daemonization originated from.
userThe user the daemonization originated from.
